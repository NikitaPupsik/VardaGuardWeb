Features

Built to catch what others miss.

Real-time threat detection

Every transaction and login is analysed the moment it happens. Suspicious patterns trigger an instant alert — before damage is done.

Behavioural analysis

ShieldApp learns what normal looks like for your accounts. Deviations in behaviour — location, timing, amount — are flagged automatically.

Device & identity verification

Detects unknown devices, impossible travel, and account takeover attempts the moment they occur.

Live dashboard

A clear, real-time overview of all activity — transactions, risk scores, and alerts — so you always know exactly what's happening.

Instant alerts

Push notifications the moment a threat is detected. High-severity events escalate automatically so nothing slips through.

Private by design

All analysis runs locally on your machine. Your data never leaves your device — no cloud processing, no third-party sharing.
